Our Floor Sanding Solihull Service<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This service relies on multiple steps and a keen eye for detail. To ensure a high-quality finish, we\u2019ll begin by preparing your existing flooring.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

To do this, we’ll use a hard-bristle brush to loosen any surface dirt or dust before vacuuming thoroughly to remove the excess. We\u2019ll also check for objects that could cause damage to our machinery and impact the overall finish of the floor. Small objects like nails and tacks will be removed. Once this first step of the process is complete, we’ll move on to sanding your floors.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Preparing your Floor<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

We’ll select the correct grit sandpaper before carefully and slowly sanding the first layer of the floor, paying close attention to detail.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Once this first sanding is complete, we\u2019ll vacuum thoroughly to remove excess dust before repeating the process once more. When sanding your floor, we\u2019re looking to achieve a surface that is lighter in colour and free from dips, blemishes or splinters. This is usually achieved with a second sand and finer grit sandpaper. However, we may decide to repeat the process a third time for best results on some floors.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Once this stage is complete, it\u2019s time to add the finish to your floor to really bring it to life.<\/p>\n\n\n
