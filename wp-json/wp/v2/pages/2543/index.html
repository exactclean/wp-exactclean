COVID-19 Coronavirus As the spread of the virus grows in the UK and around the world, people become more concerned about taking measures to protect themselves and their family.  While there are lots of ideas out there, some aren’t based in science.  However, at Exact Clean, we have a product that can help you kill the virus and works alongside the government’s official advice on combating COVID-19.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What is the COVID-19 coronavirus?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The COVID-19 coronavirus is a new strain of a virus family that we all know \u2013 it contains the common cold.  It is a new, or ‘novel’, virus that hasn’t been seen before. And that’s why so many people are being infected.  Our bodies haven’t built up an immunity to it like we have with the cold virus, and therefore we are more at risk of contracting it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

So far, the virus has been causing the most deaths among people with underlying health conditions.  It affects the lungs and airways so people with conditions like asthma and breathing problems can be worst affected.  People with lower immune systems such as diabetics are also at a higher risk of catching it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
