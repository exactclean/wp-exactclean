This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit www.exactclean.co.uk<\/p>\n

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT<\/p>\n

When you visit the Site we automatically collect information about the individual web pages that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected information as \u201cDevice Information.\u201d<\/p>\n

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:<\/p>\n

\u2013 \u201cCookies\u201d are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http:\/\/www.allaboutcookies.org.
\n\u2013 \u201cLog files\u201d track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including browser type, referring\/exit pages, and date\/time stamps.
\n\u2013 \u201cWeb beacons,\u201d \u201ctags,\u201d and \u201cpixels\u201d are electronic files used to record information about how you browse the Site.<\/p>\n

When you visit our Site and fill in a contact form, call, email or text us. We collect the customer information to be able to complete a job booking:<\/p>\n

\u2013 full customer name or business name
\n\u2013 full address and postcode
\n\u2013 contact email address
\n\u2013 phone numbers<\/p>\n

We do not store your information if no booking is made. All details are deleted.
\nWe refer to this automatically-collected information as \u201cCustomer Information.\u201d
\nWhen we talk about \u201cPersonal Information\u201d in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about Device Information and customer Information.<\/p>\n

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?<\/p>\n

We use the customer Information that we collect to fulfil any bookings placed through the Site (including providing you with invoices and\/or order confirmations). Additionally, we use this customer Information to:
\n\u2013 Communicate with you
\n\u2013 Marketing<\/p>\n

Receiving these is based on your preferences you have shared with us at point of sale. When a booking takes place at point of sale we will offer you the option to Opt in or Opt out of continuing for us to hold your information, communicating and providing marketing. This is recorded on your paperwork and an the option recorded on our electronic copies of the invoices.<\/p>\n

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site, and to assess the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns).<\/p>\n

SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION<\/p>\n

We do not share your Personal Information with third parties. Only if required to do so by law. We do use Google Analytics to help us understand how our customers use the Site\u2013you can read more about how Google uses your Personal Information here: https:\/\/www.google.com\/intl\/en\/policies\/privacy\/. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics here: https:\/\/tools.google.com\/dlpage\/gaoptout.<\/p>\n

YOUR RIGHTS<\/p>\n

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.<\/p>\n

Additionally, if you are a European resident we note that we are processing your information in order to fulfil contracts we might have with you (for example if you make an order through the Site), or otherwise to pursue our legitimate business interests listed above.<\/p>\n

DATA RETENTION<\/p>\n

When you place an order through the Sites, we will maintain your Order Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.<\/p>\n

CHANGES<\/p>\n

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.<\/p>\n
