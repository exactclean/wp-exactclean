Rinse extraction upholstery cleaning<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This is similar to the carpet rinse-extraction process. The key difference is that, with the sofa cleaning process, we use much smaller hand-held tools. These work at lower water pressures with finer spray jets to allow for thinner, more delicate fabrics.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

First we vacuum away the dry soil. We then treat the fabric with our eco-friendly cleaning solution<\/a>. Next, we rinse the fabric and then vacuum away any residue. To speed the drying process, we absorb excess residual moisture with hand held terry towels, checking our work as we go. In the case of a fabric with a nap or pile, we carefully “groom” the pile in the correct direction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

As an added option, we finish the job by applying a soil and stain protective treatment.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Low moisture, quick dry sofa cleaning Redditch<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

This process is best for upholstery which isn’t too badly soiled or stained. Typically, we use this process in the commercial environment, but with occasional applications in the home.<\/p>\n\n\n
